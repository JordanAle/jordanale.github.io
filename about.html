Industry Interests

One of my biggest industry and personal interests rests at the intersection of digital technology and agriculture. Intergration of digital technologies in the agricultural sector can lead not only to safer working conditions, and better allocation of resources, but also increased crop yield. With digital integration presenting these benefits, it makes sense to try using these technologies to address concerns of people in resource scarce and underserved communities. According to a 2019 report from the Food and Agriculture Organization of the UN, a lack of infrastructure, a lack of literacy (electronic and written), and political hurdles may prevent technologies as they exist in privledged regions from being feasible for adoption in communities that could most benefit from them. Thus, these technologies need to be developed with underserved communities' input and circumstances in mind so they too may have the option to adopt these tools. Using my background in information science, data science, agricultural production and community-centered design, I want to contribute to this sector so emerging agricultural and connective technologies can serve more people, especially those traditionally left out.
